We respect your privacy. What follows is everything Sprout Scout sees when you use it, and why.

What we process

  • Search terms — the zip code or geolocation you enter, used only to fulfil the request.
  • IP address — automatically logged by our hosting provider for security and troubleshooting; retention follows their default (~30 days).
  • Geolocation — only when you click Near me and grant your browser's location permission. The coordinates are sent to the server to find restaurants near you and are never stored.
  • Cached scan results — extracted public menu data from third-party restaurant websites, kept for up to 1 week to avoid repeated requests.

Why

To serve the search. No advertising, no profiling, no cross-site tracking, no resale of data.

Third-party processors

  • Codeberg e.V. — frontend hosting via Codeberg Pages (Berlin, Germany).
  • Render — backend hosting (United States). Transfers to a US processor are covered by the EU–U.S. Data Privacy Framework / Standard Contractual Clauses.
  • Google Fonts — typography (loaded from Google's CDN).
  • OpenStreetMap / Overpass API — restaurant directory data (ODbL-licensed, public).

Your rights

Under GDPR Articles 15–21 you may request access to, correction of, deletion or restriction of your data, object to processing, and receive a copy of any data we hold. You may also lodge a complaint with the Austrian data protection authority (Datenschutzbehörde, dsb.gv.at).
